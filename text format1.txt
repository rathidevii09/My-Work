<h3>Text Alignment</h3>
<p>The text-align property is used to set the horizontal alignment of a text.

A text can be left or right aligned, centered, or justified.</p>
<b>EXAMPLE:</b><br><br>
<h1>The text-align-last Property</h1>

<h3>Text-align-last: right:</h3>

<h3>Text-align-last: center:</h3>
